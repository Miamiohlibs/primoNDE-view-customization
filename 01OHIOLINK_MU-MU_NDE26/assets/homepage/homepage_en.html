What's Being Searched

OneSearch allows you to search for books and e-books, articles, streaming media, and more:
  • Everything searches across both library subscription databases combined with materials found in Miami University Libraries.
  • Miami Libraries Search focuses on materials found in Miami University Libraries.
  • OhioLINK Search allows you to discover and request physical items from academic libraries across Ohio.
  • Articles and More searches for articles, journals, ebooks, video, and more across Miami's subscribed databases.
  • Course Reserves searches items placed on reserve by instructors.

Search Tips

  • Focus your search: There are numerous ways to focus on the results you desire.
    • Filter by Resource Type to focus in on just books or just articles.
    • Use the location dropdown next to the search box to search just in the Miami Libraries or just at Hamilton campus library for instance.
    • Click the All Filters button for a wide selection of options from preferred language to specific academic journals.
  • Advanced Search: Two ways to create an advanced search...
    • Click the Advanced Search link next to the search bar to access a form allowing you to combine or exclude multiple search terms.
    • You can also type advanced search queries using a combination of connecting terms in allcaps and punctuation (also known as boolean searching).
      • Use AND to connect concepts
      • OR to allow search for multiple alternative terms
      • NOT to exclude terms.
      • Use parentheses to group concepts and quotation marks ("") to search for exact phrases.
      • For example: (artificial intelligence OR AI) AND ethics
  • Search beyond Miami: Turn on the "Include results outside MU Libraries" option in the filters bar to show additional resources that may not be immediately available online but could be requested via Interlibrary Loan.
  • Troubleshooting tips are available at "Off-Campus Access for Library Resources"
